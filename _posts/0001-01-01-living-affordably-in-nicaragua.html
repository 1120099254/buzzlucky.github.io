Affordably? Nicaragua is the most affordable country in Latin America -- a 1 or 2 bedroom apartment in the city center goes for about $219 and a 3-bedroom for $500 and total utilities running about $60/ month. Wifi will set you back $45/month. Live in Nicaragua and your money is worth two to three times more, especially on food, rent. For about $225/month you can have a full time housekeeper who will cook and do all your laundry...is that a luxury you have now? American expat Jack Pitman talks about his personal struggle, cost of healthcare and why he is actually happy, something that was difficult for him in the U.S.
