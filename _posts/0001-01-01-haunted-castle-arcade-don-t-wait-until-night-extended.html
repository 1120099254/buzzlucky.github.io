Other Names: Can't Wait until (the) Night
Composers: Kenichi Matsuraba, Masahiro Ikariko

The final level theme......the bridge collapsing behind Simon......the..bats doing their weird schreeches, everything turns into one.
I really like this time, and I'm happy that it was resurfaced in 2004 with Heart of Fire to create one of the best themes of the entire series.
I'm not sure which version of the name is the correct one (the second seems to be the most used, but the first is found on practically every video pre 2019).
