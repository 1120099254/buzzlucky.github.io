Army veteran Kristofer Goldsmith wages war on social-media disinformation. Since 2017, Goldsmith, who is the founder of High Ground Veterans Advocacy, has been investigating foreign entities that target troops, veterans, and their families online. He joined Hari Sreenivasan in March to discuss his work hunting fake Facebook pages that try to sow strife by deceiving veterans.

Recorded on March 6, 2020.
