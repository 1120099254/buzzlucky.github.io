Ben Lang Discusses the uses of the warmer box, a custom inclusion specifically used in the deluxe models of the Lang smoker cookers. The warmer uses the heat of the firebox below to keep food warm or to even cook. Some people find more use for it than others, so consider the benefits of the deluxe when you are making your next purchase.
