It plays at 12:30 AM each day. Most TV signoffs ended years back, but this one still occurs daily! It consists of a local ad, followed by a Fox 21 logo, a goodnight animation, our national anthem, and then a loop of the local weather. The weather loops with classical music in the background, until 6 AM, when the same National Anthem clip is played again, there is a wakeup animation, and then they play paid programming.

Edit: Recently they edited their signoff clip to cut straight to the weather loop after the logo, and not display the anthem clip/goodnight animation. How disappointing, although I'm surprised they did it this long!
