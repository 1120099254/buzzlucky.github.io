In this production tutorial, I use the elements of "Dynamite" by BTS to create my own K-Pop track.

Watch me listen to Dynamite and analyze it: https://youtu.be/FoG_3YamRrM

Everything must groove! In order to create a track like Dynamite in Logic Pro, I started with a basic drum beat playing quarter-notes. After that I ignored the grid entirely and started adding live piano, bass, and guitar to give the track a laid back feel. I love how the original BTS song drags so many elements behind the beat (the claps, the bass, and especially the vocals.)

With each element, I listened carefully to everything I had already played to make sure that it felt good. With no DAW grid to rely on, you have to turn everything over to your ear. How do you think I did? Let me know in the comments!
