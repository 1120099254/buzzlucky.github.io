A marketing hook is like a strong headline and should not be confused with a slogan. A marketing hook underlines the end result of your product or service and how fast people can potentially experience the end result. For example, should you specialize in providing natural stress relief products, your marketing hook could be: “Discover the little-known secrets to relieving stress in 30 minutes or less”. Good marketing hooks always have a big promise and an element of surprise.
