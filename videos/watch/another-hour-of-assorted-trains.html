An hour compilation of my videos that for one reason or another, I haven't used yet, that I had to put together quickly before the YouTube editor was abolished. Some older, some newer. Plus a couple extra non-train random vids thrown in. It got too complicated, so I just started using whatever was handy to fill an hour, then start again. Some of these are more organized, some are not organized at all!
