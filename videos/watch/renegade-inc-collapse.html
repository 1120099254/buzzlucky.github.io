Stephen Vizinczey says all a great power has to do to destroy itself is persist in doing the impossible...

Like bankruptcy, societies and complex systems collapse slowly then all at once. Host Ross Ashcroft is joined by journalist Indi Samarajiva to discuss how this collapse has come about.
