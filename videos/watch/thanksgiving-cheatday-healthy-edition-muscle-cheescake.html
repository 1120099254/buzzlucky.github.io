Pumpkin Peacan Muscle Cheesecake
Inspired by some different recipes but ended up doing our own thing.
Didnt really successfully make the top look the way I intended but it was descent and I learnt from my mistake.
Set your oven on 350-400F / 175-200C
Part 1:
Start with building the crust with 1 package of crackers of your choice. We thought these sounded amazing so we went for Lance Peanut butter gluten free crackers.
Smoosh them together while the butter is melting, blend it together and use a a one time foil or a form like the one that I used.
Place it in the freezer while you do part 2.

Part 2:
1 package of non fat Creme Cheese
1 egg + 1 egg white
3 tbsp of carb master or non fat greek yogurt
Mix together
Add in 1 scoop of Level 1 Pumpkin Spice Latte protein powder, 1-2 tsp of baking soda, sweetener, any spices of your liking
Blend together then add:
And 1/2 cup of pumpkin spice puree.
Take the crust out of the freezer pour over the mix and place it in the oven.

Part 3:
If you wanna have the topping that we had ( not a must ):
While baking it (depending on oven it can take 30-60 minutes to get ready - just make sure that the mix has gotten stale enough to not move when you gently push the form back and forth) make a Pecan Pie filling - a healthy one!

Use 1 egg
3 protein scoops (measuring cup) of Maple Syrup
Vanilla Powder/extract + Cinnamon
Blend it together
When the Cake has set take it out place pecans on top and then pour over the blend you just did!
Pecans amount free of choice I started with 50 g (added more when I saw that I didnt succeed with what I wanted to see :P when it was done, remember this is the one ingredient that will make the calories rise.)

Part 4:
put the cake back into the oven again and then when the topping has set - take it out and let it cool down.
Place it in your fridge overnight - then ENJOY!

Total Calories for the entire cake:
Can easily serve 8 people, which makes it 260cal per piece!!
1973 calories
