Ingredients:

1 kg chicken proben
1 tbsp salt
1 tbsp pepper
2 tbsp garlic(optional)
1/8 cup calamansi juice(optional)

Breading mix:

2 cups all purpose flour
1-2 tbsp crispy fry
Cooking oil

Sweet and Sour dip:

1 tbsp garlic, minced
1 tbsp onion, minced
1/4 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup sweet ketchup
3 tbsp brown sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup water
1 tsp cornstarch(dissolve in 2 tbsp water)
Chilis

Vinegar dip:

1 small onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, crushed
salt & pepper to taste
1/2 cup vinegar
Sugar to taste
chilis

Procedure:

1) Wash proben thoroughly 3x-4x. Drain excess water
2) Add salt, pepper, garlic and calamansi juice. Marinate for 30mins.
3) Breading mix: Combine all purpose flour and crispy fry
4) Drain the marinade and add half of the breading. Mix it well
5) Add the remaining breading mix
6) Shake the excess breading mix and Fry proben until light golden brown
7) Drain excess oil and thread proben on the bamboo skewers

Dip:

1) Sweet and Sour: Saute garlic and onion
2) Add pineapple juice, sweet ketchup, brown sugar, salt, pepper, water and chilis
3) Bring mixture to a boil
4) Add dissolved cornstarch and cook for another 2min,
5) Vinegar dip: Combine onion, garlic, salt, pepper,vinegar, sugar and chilis
