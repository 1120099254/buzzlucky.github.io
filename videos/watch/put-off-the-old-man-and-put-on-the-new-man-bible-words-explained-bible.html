What does it mean to put off the old man, and put on the new man? This may sound complicated, but it actually just comes down to a decision, and a simple act of faith. In this video in the Bible Words Explained series we explain what Paul meant by the words he wrote in Ephesians 4:22-24, and explain how you too can put off the old man, and put on the new man.
