Each year, Calgary Economic Development’s signature event, Economic Outlook, brings together business, government and community leaders to network and to hear about future economic trends and forecasts for our city, province and nation.

In 2020, we welcomed speakers Todd Hirsch, Vice President & Chief Economist, ATB Financial, and Pierre Cléroux, Vice President, Research and Chief Economist, BDC, Minister Doug Schweitzer, Minister of Jobs, Economy and Innovation, Mayor Naheed Nenshi, and Mary Moran, Chief Executive Officer at Calgary Economic Development.
