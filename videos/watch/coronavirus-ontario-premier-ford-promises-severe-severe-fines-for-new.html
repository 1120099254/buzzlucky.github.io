Ontario Premier Doug Ford on Wednesday addressed potential new social gathering rules inside the province.

Ford commented on the impending changes, and while not providing any specific details, saying there would be "severe, severe fines" for those not following the rules, and promised that they'd be the "highest in the country."

The current limit for gatherings is 50 people indoors and 100 outdoors.

Ontario reported 315 new cases of the novel coronavirus on Wednesday, bringing the provincial total to 45,383.
