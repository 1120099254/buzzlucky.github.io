What's a good printer without good paper? Nothing
There are so many types of paper out there and you will not know which one fits your printing style(s) before trying them. I learned in the last 3 years that a good quality paper makes your photos or graphic designs to pop up.
-I use (Ultra) Premium Presentation Paper 8.5x11 / 11x14 / 11.7x16.5
-HP Premium Presentation Paper 8.5x11 was my to go but it seems that they've discontinued it
Not printing photos but if I would do it, I would definetely choose the Red River Paper. How I found out what I like better? I just bought the sample box(~$14) and tried them all
