Hunter New England Health is committed to providing culturally appropriate care for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people across all community setting.

Children, young people and families across Hunter New England travel to community health facilities for variety of reasons. Some may include child development checks or for immunisations.

This video was filmed in Charlestown Square Community Health Facility.
