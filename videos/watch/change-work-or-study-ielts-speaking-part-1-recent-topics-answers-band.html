This video lesson answers the two recent IELTS Speaking Part 1 topics about Change and Work or Study.

Study how to answers the questions about change from the sample answers in the video. Make sure to use some collocations, topic vocabulary words, or some sayings such as: "Change is constant."; "Change is the only permanent thing in this world." and more.

Meanwhile, prepare how to talk about your work in the IELTS Speaking exam, if you're a worker. If you're still a student, get ideas in this video on how you can express your answers by talking about your studies.

Here are the questions:

CHANGE

WORK / STUDY

Are you studying or working?
Do you like your work?
What’s the most challenging aspect of your job?
