Sending waves of anticipation through the bass scene right now is the news that Never Say Die’s ‘Vol. 4’ compilation is on the way. Due to land on January 22nd, this nineteen-track collection is entirely made up of blistering exclusives, alongside another continuous mix from label boss SKisM.

Across this album comes a variety of new sounds from Never Say Die signings, including a suspense-filled opener from Zomboy in the form of ‘Like A Bitch’. LAXX, Megalodon, MUST DIE!, Eptic and Trampa all drop fresh material, whilst BAR9, TrollPhace and BadKlaat step up as representatives of the Black Label. Also returning, we welcome back Twine, Far Too Loud, Spag Heddy, Barely Alive and TrollPhace and brand new to NSD, epic contributions from the likes of Dr. Ozi, Carnage, Breaux, Xilent, Franky Nuts, Tisoki and Topi.

Carefully constructed and featuring the work of some of the most exciting dubstep artists out there right now, ‘Vol. 4’ is another essential compilation from Never Say Die, and one that sets 2016 up in style.
