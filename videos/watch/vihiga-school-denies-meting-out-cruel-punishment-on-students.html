Keveye Girls Secondary School in Vihiga county has refuted claims in a viral video footage showing teachers administering corporal punishment on school girls.

The footage which shows the teachers caning the girls in a packed classroom was released by one of the teachers at the school aggrieved by the brutality meted on the girls.

And as Brenda Cheruiyot reports, the school headteacher has termed the claims as malicious.
