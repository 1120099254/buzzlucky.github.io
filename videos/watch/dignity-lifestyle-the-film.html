Dignity Lifestyle is a hassle-free retirement township project by Dignity Foundation for active and productive living for senior citizens. It is located amongst green environment near the picturesque hill station of Matheran, at Neral,just 90 kms from Mumbai.

Dignity Lifestyle offers you an opportunity to:

1. Live with total security
2. In unpolluted, quiet, clean environment
3. Drive away the "Empty Nest" syndrome
4. Engage in social work
5. Take part in development of nearby villages
6. Showcase your talent
