The new Kia Telluride is big, bold and boxy! It's the new midsize SUV of Kia and it's made for adventures! It's the largest Kia ever that provides comfortable seating for up to eight occupants (includes the driver)! The Kia Telluride is using the potent 3.8-liter GDI V6 with 291 horsepower and 262 lb.-ft. of torque! The Telluride has a powerful exterior design and premium, well-appointed interior! It's also loaded with technology to enhance comfort, convenience, utility and the driver and passenger experience! It's also equipped with active on-demand all-wheel drive! This AWD system helps provide enhanced driving performance by actively distributing torque between the front and rear wheels depending on road conditions and driver input.
