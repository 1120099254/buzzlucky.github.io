Police and metro police in Cape Town, stopped motorists along Jan van Riebeeck Road near Eerste River on the first day of the 21 day nationwide lockdown.
They asked motorists to provide permits or valid reasons for being on the road. They also checked the number of passengers per vehicle.
Minister of Transport Fikile Mbalula said that the number of passengers would be reduced during the lockdown.
