"SEWT was born from a simple idea – we wear suits too. A female-founded company, SEWT stands for Suits Especially for Women Tailored. Our mission is to be part of the story of the womxn who inspire us. We do this by crafting suits especially made for women, and by aligning ourselves with initiatives that move women forward."
