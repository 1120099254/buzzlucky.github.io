H.E.A.T RELEASE NEW SINGLE & VIDEO "RISE"
Recently, H.E.A.T announced their brand-new album “H.E.A.T II” which is set to be released early next year. “H.E.A.T II” is the first album to be produced entirely by the band - with Jona Tee and Dave Dalone as producers. “This is what we would sound like if we made a debut album 2019, hence the title.”
The recent single “One By One” (released September 27th) gave a first glance into what is to come for the new album, followed today by the anthem “Rise”.
The band explains: “Rise is about uniting and rebelling against the powers that be. Whatever that may be is subjective to the listener.”
