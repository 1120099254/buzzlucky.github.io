How would you like to be able to head off an emotional reaction in an instant? Mark Youngblood, founder and CEO of Inner Mastery Inc,. introduces "Power Phrases" which you can use to transform an emotional reaction in the blink of an eye. This technique is explored in more detail in his new book, Dear Human, Master Your Emotions.

Being able to "Pivot to Power," which is shifting from an emotional reaction to a powerful response is key to success in every aspect of life. Power phrases work on the basis of "classical conditioning" which was first described by Ivan Pavlov. In essence, you exchange a trigger that leads to an emotional reaction for one the leads to a positive response by taking it to a "Power Phrase" that you memorize.
