Announcing Evensong: A new project and album from Keith & Kristyn Getty
A collection of hymns and lullabies at the close of day - available August 28!

Born out of the thoughts, songs, and prayers the Gettys use to close each day with their own family, this new project features a mixture of lullabies and hymns to help redirect the mind and comfort the heart. Historically, the Church has known this practice as Evensong, and it is our hope that believers around the world might use these songs with their families and by themselves especially in this unique season of life.

Feat. guest appearances from VINCE GILL, HEATHER HEADLEY, ELLIE HOLCOMB, SIERRA HULL, DEBORAH KELMME, AND SANDRA McCRACKEN.

Debut concert and album release online at Sing! Global. Join thousands of believers around the globe in singing together for the world's largest Evensong celebration! https://gettymusicworshipconference.com/
