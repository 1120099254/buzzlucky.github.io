The Ibis Mojo has been a staple in the Ibis catalogue since way back in 1994 when it debuted as a hardtail. The latest version features an all new frame and geometry, but how does that perform out on the trail?

Note: After testing was completed we learned that our bike came with a 2020 Fox 34 FIT 4 fork due to bike boom related product shortages. The correct spec, and the way the bike is shipping now, is with a 2021 Factory 34 with a GRIP 2 damper.
