Random Notions Production In associated with Limelyte Films presents to you Soch - The Required Change.

This story revolves around the concept of 'Women Empowerment'.
It's a short Story about how Sanjana explains her wish of working even after marriage to Dharmik in a sarcastic manner rather than leaving from there. This no only changes Dharmik's opinion about Women Empowerment but also makes him realize his mistakes.

Through this Short film We the members of Random Notions Would Like to give a message to the society to support Women Empowerment and Agree to the fact that all Men and Women are equal and should be given equal opportunity to fulfill their desire.
Support the Movement by Using the hashtag #TimeToChangeOurSOCH
