Periods are normal and a sign of health. They are just one part of a natural process known as the menstrual cycle (or monthly cycle). The cycle is a way for the body to prepare itself for pregnancy. An average menstrual cycle lasts 28 days (but anything from 21 to 35 is considered normal).

The menstrual cycle has 4 phases:
1. Menstruation
2. Pre-ovulation
3. Ovulation
4. Pre-menstruation
