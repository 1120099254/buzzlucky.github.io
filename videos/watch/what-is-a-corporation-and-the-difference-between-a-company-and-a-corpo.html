In the video, I ask Steve what is the difference between a company and a corporation. The term company is just a generic term. A corporation is a company that gives the people who own and work for that company limited liability protection. That means people are not personally liable for any of the acts of the corporation.

If you are wondering what is a corporation, you get limited liability protection almost no matter what kind of business entity you open (LLC, S corp, C corp).
