Yay, Little Kitten is back and so excited about going to primary school! Our app „Little Kitten & Friends“ helps preparing children aged between 4 and 7 for their school start. Training perception, coordination and cognitive abilities - with Little Kitten this is a lot of fun!

In 8 educational games, children learn social and motor skills. The teacher Ms. Fox lovingly guides the children through their first lessons. Confident children and parents without worries - we'll get you ready for school!


Little Kitten Preschool Learning Games:

8 adorable mini games where you and Little Kitten solve puzzles and tasks together.

1.Animal Shadow Game:

Bat, crocodile or elephant? Do you recognize animals only by their shape? This game helps to enhance cognitive abilities and grows in difficulty as the game progresses.

2.Paint Game:

Color in the animal shapes but don't go over the edge! This game is about creativity and fine motor skills - the various forms increase with difficulty and require hand-eye coordination to stay within the shape, this control helps to improve children’s writing skills.


3.Trampoline Game:

What trick is Little Kitten thinking of? Be quick to recognize the trick and tell the others so they can pull it off together! A game for improving visual perception and reaction speed.

4.Bubble Game:

Match the pictures to the themes! What can you eat and what shouldn't you? This game is about logic and pattern recognition.

5.Recycling Game:

Learn to sort and recycle various types of rubbish and develop a sense of responsibility. Kids love the funny monster bins!

6.Hop Scotch Game:

Help Little Kitten hop in the right order! Do you remember it? This game enhances concentration, sound and visual memory. There are Easy, Medium & Hard modes to provide the right amount of challenge for every child.

7.Count & Sums Game:

How many arms does an octopus have? How many is it missing? Learn to count from 1 to 10 and to perceive amounts visually - which pile looks bigger? Children start to develop mathematical skills, an important part of their cognitive ability.

8.AR Game (only for AR compatible devices):

Find the crackers in Little Kitten’s Box! Spatial awareness and coordination will be improved.

Other Features:

•Feeding Game: Each mini game has a unique cracker reward, play all the learning games to collect them and feed them to Kitten to watch Kitten's special reaction to each!

•Voice Acted in 14 Languages!: Touch the world map and choose from any of 14 supported languages, now Miss Fox's voice will talk you through every mini-game in your chosen language, you can even give her a tap to hear a tip any time. Once your child knows all the mini-games in their native language this is a great way for them to pick up words in a new language!
