I was recently asked a question close to this: "How can I create a constraint such that respondents can’t select “None of the Above” and another option. I want to create a constraint that prevents selecting “None of the Above” and any other option from a multiple_select question.”

This applies when you're building your form in XLSForm or in KoboToolbox, for example.

This is such a good question. And it is very common in humanitarian and development questionnaires, where we give a list of options and say “select ALL that apply”. And then at the bottom of the list, we might have options like:
- Not applicable
- I don’t know
- None of the above

But it wouldn’t make sense if the questionnaire allowed you to select “None of the above” with another option also.

So…there is a great constraint that you can use in your Kobo Toolbox forms to prevent this from happening.
