The ending credits track from Wes Anderson's "Grand Budapest Hotel" (2014) It is an arrangement of a famous Russian folksong called "Светит месяц" or "The Moon Is Shining." Some mistake the bright sound of the stringed instruments to be that of mandolins but they are Russian balalaikas.
