In this video I will show you how to use both 'beside' and 'besides' in a sentence. These are two often-misused and misunderstood connectors. So what is the meaning of the English linking word 'Besides'? How is it different from 'beside' and exactly how do we use it in a sentence? In this video we'll be looking at answers to these questions and I'll give you lots of examples. This will be useful if you're studying for a Cambridge exam such as IELTS, First Certificate (FCE), Advanced (CAE) or Proficiency (CPE).
