Greece's ongoing financial crisis has severely damaged small businesses in the country. The country's Chamber of Commerce and Industry said more than 100 stores have been shut down since 2008, while over one million people have lost their jobs. High taxes and low wages limit people’s spending that they can hardly make ends meet. CGTN’s Eirini Zarkadoula has more.
