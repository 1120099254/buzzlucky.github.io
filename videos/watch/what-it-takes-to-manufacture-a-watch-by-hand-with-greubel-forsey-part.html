We are extremely happy to share with you a behind the scene journey into the making of Greubel Forsey's Hand Made 1 timepiece. This project not only pays tribute to the very origin of mechanical watchmaking when the hand was the most precious tool used, but also achieving a spectacular end result in terms of performance and level of precision corresponding to today's standards.

Meet Robert Greubel and Stephen Forsey, founders of the brand, and all the team behind this amazing project, discover their motivation, the challenges encountered and the solutions found.
