Ingredients:
1 cup of sugar
1/2 cup of water
1 tablespoon of lemon juice
1 teaspoon of ground ginger
few cloves
1 tablespoon of honey

For this homemade cough remedy all you need to do is mix all ingredients and boil them for about 20 minutes on low heat. It is ready when it is thick like honey. Then make little drops, and when they are cold and harden cover them in powdered sugar. Store this homemade cough remedy in container and use for curing sore throat, cough and flue.
Warning! Do not use this cough remedy if you have any allergies on honey or ginger or pollen. Use this on your own responsibility.
If you have any serious symptoms see your doctor.
