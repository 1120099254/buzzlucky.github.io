lets Flashback over The career of Gelo

LiAngelo Ball attended Chino Hills High School and became part of the team in the 2013-14 season. Gelo contributed to Chino Hills reaching their first-ever CIF Southern Section Open Division championship game.After the season ended, he verbally committed to UCLA. He was their first verbal commit of the 2017’s class. In his junior season, gelo was now playing with both of his brothers, Lonzo and Melo. that season he averaged 27.4 points per game, which led his team. Senior season he scored a career-high 72 points with 13 three-pointers. After a 30–3 season, he was named All-Area Player of the Year. he was also a first-team All-State and honorable mention All-American selection; he averaged 33.8 points per game.


November 7, 2017, days before UCLA's regular season opener he and two other freshman teammates were arrested for allegedly stealing sunglasses from a Louis Vuitton store near their team hotel. On December 4, he announced that he planned to withdraw from UCLA LaVar had grown frustrated over the length of the suspension. Although he was not considered a prospect at the time for the NBA, he planned on preparing for the 2018 draft with his father after leaving UCLA. On December 11, 2017, he signed with Prienai in Lithuanian,along with his younger brother Melo.Gelo would finish that season averaging 12.6 points and 2.7 rebounds per game, shooting 41.5% from the three-point line.


Gelo signed with the Los Angeles Ballers of the JBA, a league founded by his father as an alternative to college basketball.At the end of the season, Ball was among 14 players in the league named to the JBA USA select Team, which faced several European teams on an international tour.
