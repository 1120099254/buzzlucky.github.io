John Gibbons is a registered Osteopath, Lecturer and Author and is demonstrating how to correct an extended right rotated and right side bent L5 facet on S1, this is called an ERS(R) and is a type 2 spinal mechanic dysfunction. John corrects the spinal dysfucntion by using a muscle energy technique (MET).

John also discusses the position of the iliac crest regarding leg length, anterior / posterior rotation and upslip of the innominate bone. This technique and more is taught on the Pelvis, SIJ and Lumbar spine Master-Class at the University of Oxford.
